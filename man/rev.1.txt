     rreevv -- reverse lines of a file or files

SSYYNNOOPPSSIISS
     rreevv [--VV] [--hh] [_f_i_l_e _._._.]

DDEESSCCRRIIPPTTIIOONN
     The rreevv utility copies the specified files to standard output, reversing
     the order of characters in every line.  If no files are specified, stan-
     dard input is read.

     --VV,, ----vveerrssiioonn
             Output version information and exit.

     --hh,, ----hheellpp
             Output help and exit.
